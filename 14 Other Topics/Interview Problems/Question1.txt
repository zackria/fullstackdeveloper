Facebook Interview Question

You are given a function strongPassword(password)

Somebody enters a password into your function 

You have to figure out what is the minimum number of edits necessary to make it a “strong” password 

A strong password has at least one number, one uppercase letter is at least 6 characters long but less than 20 characters and 3 of the same letter cannot repeat in a row 

An edit is adding a letter, removing a letter, or replacing a letter 

You have 45 minutes to figure out
